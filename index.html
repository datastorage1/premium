Welcome Note

Dear Cricket Enthusiasts,

We are delighted to invite you to the MP Club Plastic Ball Cricket Tournament 2025, a special event organized to celebrate Eid-Ul-Ajha. This tournament promises to be an exciting showcase of talent, sportsmanship, and community spirit in the unique format of plastic ball cricket.

Join us for an unforgettable experience of competitive cricket, where teams will battle for glory in a friendly yet competitive environment. With attractive prize money and the opportunity to showcase your skills, this tournament is not to be missed!

Invitation Letter

To Whom It May Concern,

We cordially invite you to participate in the MP Club Plastic Ball Cricket Tournament 2025, scheduled to be held on June 8th, 2025. This tournament is being organized to celebrate the spirit of Eid-Ul-Ajha and promote sportsmanship in our community through the exciting format of plastic ball cricket.

Tournament Details:

  • Date: June 8th, 2025
  • Venue: Kumbhira (In front Of Kumbhira Abashik Mission)
  • Team Size: 9 Players per Team
  • Format: 4 Overs per Team
  • Entry Fee: ₹301 per Team

We look forward to your participation and wish you the best of luck!

Best Regards,

MP Club Tournament Committee
